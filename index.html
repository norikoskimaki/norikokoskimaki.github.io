About Me.

Graphic Designer / Web Development

Hello, I'm Noriko Koskimaki, originally from Japan and moved to Vancouver, Canada in 2009. I have worked as an administrator for various companies and fields for more than 10 years, and I studied Graphic Design and Web Development programs at BCIT. While my passion is lean on design, I find it is fun to put my design into websites using my programming skills.

In 2019, I moved to Kelowna with my family and started working freelance basis.

While being a mom of 2 children, I enjoy my spare time to listen music, watch & go skating, and gardening.
